# Twilio A2P 10DLC Registration — ESEE Enterprises

Use these answers when completing the Twilio A2P 10DLC campaign registration.

---

## Campaign Description

ESEE Enterprises sends SMS messages to clients who have opted in to receive communications related to our AI automation and business consulting services. Messages include appointment reminders, scheduling confirmations, service status updates, and important business notifications. All recipients have provided prior express consent to receive messages. Message frequency varies based on service interactions, up to 10 messages per month. Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

---

## How Do Recipients Consent to Receive Messages?

Recipients provide express written consent by submitting their mobile phone number through our online intake forms, scheduling tools, or service agreements. Each consent point includes clear disclosure that the recipient is opting in to receive SMS messages from ESEE Enterprises, states the types of messages they will receive (appointment reminders, service updates, and business notifications), provides a link to our Privacy Policy (https://eseeent.com/privacy) and Terms & Conditions (https://eseeent.com/terms), and states that consent is not a condition of purchase. Consent records including the date, time, and method of opt-in are stored and retained for compliance purposes.

---

## Opt-In Workflow

1. The client engages with ESEE Enterprises through a consultation request, intake form, or service agreement.
2. During intake, the client provides their mobile phone number in a form field accompanied by the following disclosure: "By providing your mobile number, you consent to receive SMS messages from ESEE Enterprises including appointment reminders, service updates, and business notifications. Message frequency varies, up to 10 msgs/month. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions."
3. The client must actively check a consent checkbox or sign the agreement to opt in. Pre-checked boxes are not used.
4. Upon opt-in, the client receives a confirmation SMS: "ESEE Enterprises: You're subscribed to appointment reminders & service updates. Msg frequency varies, up to 10 msgs/mo. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out."
5. Consent records are logged with a timestamp and opt-in source.
